Hypersensitivity

Hypersensitivity in dogs refer to an allergic reaction that happened in dogs which the immune system identifies allergen as threat. This causes the inflammation, itching and other uncomfortable symptoms. There are several types of the allergic reaction in dogs such as skin (atopic) allergies, food allergies, flea allergy dermatitis and others. There are many factors contributing to this hypersensitivity and the severity level depends on the extent and nature of the reaction.

Symptoms

Red, inflamed, or flaky skin

Hair loss or hot spots

Ear infections or head shaking

Sudden facial or throat swelling or having difficulty breathing or wheezing

Prevention

Maintaining a clean environment for dogs to live in by carrying out regular cleaning, air filtration to reduce environmental allergens

Ensuring a good diet and nutrition by feeding high quality meals to dog and handling food that dog is allergic to with caution

Consult with veterinarian the parasite control products that are safe to be applied on dogs
